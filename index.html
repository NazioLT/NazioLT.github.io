About me

I'm Henno Théo, game-design student at E-Artsup Lille in 2nd year and self-educated programmer. I'm passionated by video games since my childhood, not only playing them, but also creating and programming them.



So that's why, from the age of 13, I started to learn how to make a video games. I started on Unity 3D with UnityScript then with c# as a self educated, but I didn't spend a lot of time on it.

But as the years passed, the more time I spent learning programming and creating video games. So in 2021, I started my new studies at E-Artsup in the GameDesign section, for 5 years.
